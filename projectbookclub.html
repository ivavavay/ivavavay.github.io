Design Book Club

Time frame: Jan 2019 - Present

Project Partner: Christina Cook



Introduction

It is often difficult to find good design books. Our goal is to create a collection of design books recommended by the design community

Who Are We Designing For?

People in the design community who like to read books!

Problems We Want to Solve

-It is difficult to know which books are considered essential readings when you first step foot into the design scene.

-Popular articles are often based on classic/widely acclaimed design books. However, they’re not often credited when referenced.

-People often have to ask specific people within their networks to ask for books on a specific design topic, i.e. color theory, because there isn’t a collective archive of these books by topics.
