How can we better appreciate the guest speakers who travel from afar to come to our classes?

We believe in the value of thanking people, for taking the time out of their day to come down to Santa Cruz and empower us with their inspirational talks. To show the depths of our appreciation, we designed a Thank You note website to send to each and every one of the speakers.
