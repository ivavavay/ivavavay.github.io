about

Hailing from the town of Dusseldorf, Germany, I was previously a second-year studying Cognitive Science w/ a concentration in human-computer interaction at the University of California, Santa Cruz. At college, I spent most of my time designing interfaces at the Tech4Good lab. Currently, I am designing remotely in the Bay Area for a project within the lab.

I wish to learn more about the journalism industry and how we can better design for readers to collectively make contributions to issues they care about. Additionally, I am interested in exploring how we could better design systems that distribute scalable medical, financial, and technological resources to people who have been historically underserved and marginalized, locally and globally. I strive to be research-grounded and ethic-based in the design projects that I've had the fortune of working on. :)
