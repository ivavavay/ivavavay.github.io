about

Hailing from the town of Dusseldorf, Germany, I was previously a second-year studying Cognitive Science w/ a concentration in human-computer interaction at the University of California, Santa Cruz. At college, I spent most of my time designing interfaces at the Tech4Good lab.

Currently, I am designing remotely in the Bay Area for a project within the lab. I am interested in exploring how we could better design systems that distribute technological resources to people who have been historically underserved and marginalized, locally and globally. Additionally, I am passionate about protecting the environment, and I wish to integrate an environmentally-conscious approach into every aspect of my work. As a designer, I strive to be research-grounded and ethic-based in the projects that I've had the fortune of working on. :)
