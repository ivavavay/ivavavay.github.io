I'm a second-year cognitive science student at UC Santa Cruz.


Hailing from the town of Dusseldorf, I am a German-born Taiwanese-Canadian who currently resides in Santa Cruz, California. At UCSC, I spend most of my time designing interfaces at the Tech4Good lab. I founded a human-centered design and development club - Web and Mobile with my friend Greg, though he honestly did most of the work. On campus, I am involved with the Tiny House Micropod Project and play on the Women's Club Ultimate Frisbee Team. Go Sol!
